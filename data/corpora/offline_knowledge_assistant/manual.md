# Local Operations Manual

Site: North Ridge Water Recovery Plant
Operational Domain: municipal water operations
Primary Asset: HX-47 booster pump (HX-47)

## Known Condition

The recurring condition covered here is persistent vibration after a seal replacement paired with a suction-pressure drop from 4.1 bar to 2.8 bar.

## First-Line Checks

- re-prime the pump before any further run time
- inspect for suction-side air ingress around the seal housing and intake couplings
- confirm the suction strainer is not partially blocked

## Prohibited Shortcut

Do not increase motor speed above 92 percent to compensate for the pressure drop.

## Escalation Trigger

Escalate when the vibration persists longer than 5 minutes after re-priming and strainer checks.
