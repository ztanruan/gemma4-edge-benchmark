# Recent Site Notes

- Operators reported persistent vibration after a seal replacement paired with a suction-pressure drop from 4.1 bar to 2.8 bar on HX-47.
- The first successful recovery started by re-prime the pump before any further run time.
- The wrong response in a prior incident was to increase motor speed above 92 percent to compensate for the pressure drop.
- If the issue persists, contact Elena Park (on-shift rotating equipment technician).
- The current part reference is STR-KIT-447; stock note says 2 kits available in maintenance cage B14.
