# Payer Policy Excerpt

Request Type: lumbar MRI prior authorization
Primary issue: payer requires either six weeks of conservative therapy or documented red-flag symptoms

## Required Administrative Checks

- verify the patient plan and imaging benefit requirements
- attach physical-therapy notes documenting conservative treatment duration
- screen for red-flag symptoms that move the case to urgent review

## Do Not Default To

Do not default to scheduling a contrast MRI when the policy only supports standard lumbar MRI.
